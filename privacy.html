Privacy Policy

Privacy is essential, permanent and non-negotiable.

The team here at Meanwise are personally invested in creating a platform designed from the ground up with privacy at its core. Every design decision undergoes a rigorous privacy review process. We were the first users of Meanwise ourselves and we wanted to design and iterate a product that we would feel is safe for our families and friends to use.

Our guiding principle is unambiguous. When you use Meanwise, you choose

i) how and when you will express yourself

ii) your audience.

What information does Meanwise collect?

-- Information you choose to give us

-- Information we get when you use our services

-- Third parties that send us information.

Data you choose to give us:

Meanwise requires you to set up a basic account, which requires details such as: username, password, email address, phone number and date of birth. Other information may include: a profile picture or video. You will always be asked for your consent before entering in information.

We also collect data when you contact our support team so as to improve our service.

Information we get when you use our services

  • Usage information
  • Content information
  • Device information
  • Device addressbook
  • Camera and photos
  • Location information
  • Cookies and other technologies
